Investment policy statement purpose

This Investment Policy Statement (IPS) provides the framework for managing your investment assets. It reflects your investment goals and objectives and will be used to guide the strategy and administration of your Custom Managed Portfolios.

The recommendations outlined in this IPS are a result of careful examination of your current financial position, future investment goals, liquidity requirements, need for current income, long-term portfolio expectations, and ability to withstand market fluctuations.

This assessment helps define the appropriate allocation of your investments across various asset classes. Once a suitable asset allocation is determined, an appropriate investment strategy is then developed for your optimal portfolio.

Although great care has been taken to ensure the accuracy of this report, keep in mind that projections, by their very nature, are based on a variety of assumptions and as such it is likely that the actual results achieved will be somewhat different than illustrated. For this reason, it is very important that you and your team of investment professionals review your investment needs and objectives on a regular basis to ensure they are current and relevant to your changing financial position.

Client-directed holdings

In addition to the securities purchased in your managed accounts according to the recommended investment models, the following account(s) will include non-model holdings based on your direction. These non-model holdings are non-discretionary positions integrated into your managed accounts. They will be held in a separate sleeve carved out within the account ("Client-directed sleeve") and will not affect the management and performance of the intended investment strategies.

No trading will occur in the Client-directed sleeves without your explicit direction. All trading instructions will be communicated to the Managed Assets Program trading team by your investment advisor and executed with the same diligence and standards as model assets.

Assets in the Client-directed sleeves will not be considered in the suitability assessment of the recommended portfolio mix above. Your investment advisor will collaborate with you to ensure these non-model holdings align with your financial goals.

Brookfield Private Real Assets Fund

Brookfield Private Real Assets Fund L.P. Canada ("BPRAF") offers a comprehensive solution for accessing Brookfield's premier private real estate and infrastructure expertise, focusing on value creation strategies. BPRAF's investments in private real assets aim to provide investors with:

  1. Attractive total returns
  2. Low volatility
  3. Inflation protection

BPRAF is designed to deliver the benefits of private real assets without the operational complexities typically associated with traditional private funds. Key features of BPRAF include operational ease, quarterly liquidity, and a diversified portfolio.

BPRAF is continuously offered with quarterly liquidity, subject to a 90-day notice period. However, there is no secondary market for BPRAF, and none is expected to develop. Therefore, this investment is suitable for investors who do not require immediate or short-term liquidity. Liquidity refers to how quickly and easily an asset can be sold and converted into cash. During volatile markets, such as periods of sudden interest rate changes, certain securities may become less liquid, meaning they cannot be sold quickly or easily. Some securities may be illiquid due to legal restrictions, the nature of the investment, specific features (such as guarantees), or a lack of interested buyers. Difficulty in selling securities may lead to higher volatility and potential losses or reduced returns.

Redemptions are subject to a 5% fund-level gate and may be placed in a redemption queue. A fund-level gate limits redemptions temporarily to prevent a run on the fund during market stress, allowing redemptions to occur at a pace that minimizes impact on BPRAF's value, liquidity, and concentration.

Redemptions are processed in two stages: 80% of the redemption amount is paid approximately 30 days after the redemption date, with the remaining balance paid once the net asset value (NAV) is finalized. For additional details, please refer to the latest Brookfield Private Real Asset Fund Capital Activity Dates.

Hamilton Lane Global Private Assets Fund

Hamilton Lane Global Private Assets Fund ("HLGPAF") provides investors with streamlined access to Hamilton Lane's global expertise in private assets, focusing on diverse strategies such as buyouts, venture capital, and private debt. HLGPAF seeks to generate attractive long-term capital appreciation by investing in a wide range of private assets globally, while maintaining liquidity for periodic redemptions.

  1. Global diversification across various private market strategies
  2. Professional management by experienced private markets investors
  3. Potential for enhanced returns compared to traditional public market investments

HLGPAF's portfolio emphasizes diversification, liquidity, and risk management, avoiding the complexities of traditional private fund structures. The key benefits to investors include attractive capital appreciation potential and limited liquidity options through periodic redemption windows. HLGPAF is suitable for investors seeking medium- to long-term investments in private assets and is not appropriate for those requiring immediate or frequent liquidity.

Liquidity in HLGPAF is limited and subject to certain restrictions. While HLGPAF offers monthly liquidity, redemptions may be subject to gates, suspensions, or delays under certain circumstances. Investors should be prepared to hold their investment for an extended period. The value of private market investments may be difficult to determine and may fluctuate more widely than publicly traded securities.

For more detailed information on HLGPAF's structure, risks, and terms, please refer to the official Fund documents and consult with your investment advisor.

Investment policy statement agreement

The purpose of this Investment Policy Statement (IPS) is to guide your team of Investment Professionals in effectively implementing and overseeing a strategy for managing your investment assets. This document establishes your expectations and objectives, outlining the asset allocation and investment strategies to be employed in the execution of your IPS.

This IPS serves as an effective tool for confirming communications between you and your team of Investment Professionals, ensuring that important issues and concerns are clearly addressed. You will receive monthly statements if transactions have occurred within your account, and quarterly statements detailing the performance of each Investment Manager Strategy within your portfolio.

Since this IPS is based on your responses to the initial questionnaire, any changes in your needs or circumstances may necessitate a different asset and manager allocation. We recommend discussing your investment needs and circumstances with your Investment Professional at least once a year.

By agreeing to this document, the potential for conflicts and misunderstandings is significantly reduced. Please sign this Agreement to confirm your agreement with its contents. Note that this Investment Policy Statement is not a contract; it is intended to summarize the agreed-upon investment strategy for your portfolio.
