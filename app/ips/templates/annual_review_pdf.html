Purpose for an annual review

The purpose of the annual review is to revisit the fundamentals of your Investment Policy Statement (IPS). It is necessary for us to review your investment philosophy, objectives, risk tolerances and time horizon to ensure that your portfolio is aligned to meet these needs.

Below you will find a summary of the information which you had previously provided in the questionnaire and the basis for our previous IPS recommendation. If any of this information has changed, then your asset allocation may need to be updated to reflect the changes. Please provide us with any feedback you believe is necessary in order for the Portfolio Manager to make a more informed decision in regard to your current asset allocation needs.
